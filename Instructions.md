# Vacation Planner App
## Purpose:
This app is designed for users to track and plan their vacation. The user will be able to create vacation plans while attaching excursion associated with said vacation. This app provides the opportuntiy to edit and delete vacations and excursions as well as set alerts when the big day is coming starting and ending. User will also be able to share the details of the vacation to others via text message, clipboard or email.

## Directions:

1. On homepage, tap on "Start Planning"
1. On the Vacation List screen, tab the "Plus" in the bottom right hand corner to add a vacation.
1. Begin entering your vacation details:
    1. Vacation Title
   2. Where you will be staying
   3. Start Date of the Vacation
   4. End date of the Vacation
   5. Tap Save Vacation
1. After tapping on Save Button your app will direct you back to the vacation list. You have now created your first vacation!
1. In the Vacation List page you may tap on any Vacation saved to take you to the Vacation Detail page, where you can:
   1. Edit or delete vacations.
   2. Tap on the menu buttons in the top right hand corner, Tap "Add Start Alert" to notify you when the Vacation is starting.
   2. Tap on the menu buttons in the top right hand corner, Tap  "Add End Alert" to notify you when the Vacation is ending.
   3. Tap on the menu buttons in the top right hand corner, Tap "Share" to copy the details of your vacation to a clip board and paste them in a text message or e-mail.

1. To add excursions to existing vacation, tap on the vacation you created in the Vacation list screen. 
   4. Tap on the "Plus" in the bottom right hand corner.
4. Begin entering your excursion details:
    1. Excursion Title
   2. Excursion Date
   3. Tap Save Excursion
1. You now have saved an excursion associated with this vacation and you should see the details under Excursions at the bottom. 
1. In the Vacation Detail page you may tap on the excursion to bring you to the Excursion Detail page, where you can:
    1. Edit or delete excursions
   2. Add alert of the excursion date, tap the menu buttons in the top right hand corner and select "Set Alert"
